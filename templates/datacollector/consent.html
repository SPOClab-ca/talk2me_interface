Research Project

The Computational Linguistics group at the Department of Computer Science in the University of Toronto is conducting a study to analyze the differences in longitudinal progression of linguistic markers between healthy older adults and ones with dementia. We are interested in assessing the validity and usability of an automated web-based and phone-based system ({{ website_name }}) for linguistic data acquisition, as means for longitudinal monitoring of changes in language ability.
